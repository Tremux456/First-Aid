Introduction to First Aid for Third-Degree Burns

These burns destroy the epidermis and dermis layers of the skin, and may damage underlying bones, muscles, and tendons. The skin may appear white, charred, or leathery, and there might be no pain due to nerve damage2.

First Aid Steps:

  1. Call 112 immediately
  2. Do not remove clothing stuck to the burn
  3. Do not soak the burn in water or apply ointments
  4. Cover the burn with a cool, wet sterile bandage or clean cloth
  5. Elevate burned limbs to reduce swelling
  6. Treat for shock if necessary
