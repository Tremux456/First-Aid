First Aid for Nose bleeding

A nosebleed, also known as epistaxis, can occur when the tiny blood vessels inside the nose are damaged :

Click for Translation

# Kutoka damu Puani, pia inajulikana kama epistaxis, inaweza kutokea wakati mishipa midogo ya damu ndani ya pua imeharibiwa

    Steps for Dealing with a Nosebleed.
  1. Stay Calm:

    2. Try to stay composed and keep the person calm.

    Click for Translation

    # Jaribu kukaa na kumtuliza mtu huyo kwa utulivu.

  2. Sit and Lean Forward:

    3. Have the person sit down and slightly lean forward. This prevents blood from entering the throat and helps to avoid choking.

    Click for Translation

    # Acha mtu huyo kukaa chini na kuegemea kidogo mbele. Hii inazuia damu kuingia kwenye koo na husaidia kuzuia kuvuta.

  3. Pinch the Nostrils:

    4. Use your thumb and forefinger to pinch the nostrils together, just below the bony part of the nose. Hold for about 5-10 minutes.

    Click for Translation

    # Tumia kidole chako na paji la uso ili kubana pua pamoja, chini ya sehemu ya bony ya pua. Shikilia kwa karibu dakika 5-10.

  4. Breathe Through the Mouth:

    5. Encourage the person to breathe through their mouth while pinching the nose.

    Click for Translation

    # Mhimize mtu kupumua kupitia kinywa chao wakati wa kubana pua.

  5. Avoid Lying Down:

    6. This can cause blood to flow down the throat, which can be dangerous.

    Click for Translation

    # Usilale chini, Hii inaweza kusababisha damu kutiririka koo, ambayo inaweza kuwa hatari.

  6. Apply Cold Compress (Optional):

    7. Place an ice pack or cold compress on the bridge of the nose to help constrict blood vessels.

    Click for Translation

    # Weka pakiti ya barafu au compress baridi kwenye daraja la pua ili kusaidia mishipa ya damu.

    When to Seek Medical Help
  1. If the bleeding doesn't stop after 20 minutes.
  2. If the nosebleed occurs frequently.
  3. If it’s caused by a significant injury.
  4. If the person is taking blood-thinning medication or has a bleeding disorder.
Click for Translation
    # Wakati wa kutafuta msaada wa matibabu
  1. Ikiwa kutokwa na damu hakuachi baada ya dakika 20.
  2. Ikiwa utokea mara kwa mara.
  3. Ikiwa imesababishwa na jeraha kubwa.
  4. Ikiwa mtu anachukua dawa ya kupunguza damu au ana shida ya kutokwa na damu.
